Visit us on Saturday June 22nd to find out why we are now counted among the top 400 universities in the world (THE world university rankings). Copies of the timetable will be available on the day (no need to print!)

REGISTER - SAT JUNE 22 2019

SPRING OPEN DAY 2019 TIMETABLE - JUNE TIMETABLE WILL BE POSTED SOON

Start planning your visit to to our Open Day on Saturday June 22nd. We are open from 10.30am-3pm.

  • Throughout the Open Days our student ambassadors lead guided tours of the campus and facilities, and are available to answer all of your questions about student life here.
  • In the Iontas, John Hume, Eolas and new School of Education Buildings, our lecturers are on hand to discuss the finer details of the courses that interest you.
  • Talks on a diverse range of subjects from psychology, to primary teaching, to careers and subject choice throughout the Open Days.
  • Once you've registered you'll get a voucher for a free hot drink (tea/filter coffee).

Open Day View Spring Timetable

REGISTER - SAT JUNE 22ND 2019

SPRING OPEN DAY 2019 TIMETABLE - JUNE TIMETABLE WILL BE PUBLISHED SOON

Don't forget to register for the Open Day to enjoy a free hot drink during your visit (tea/filter coffee).

  • Grab an Open Day booklet, timetable or download our free events app when you arrive on campus (free wifi - select Open Day).
  • Plan the talks you'll attend so you don't miss out - degree talks, information for mature students, parents and supports provided by the University and many more.
  • Meet students from some of the clubs and societies.
  • Talk to us. Ask us questions and leave the campus feeling sure you've got the most out of your visit!

Travelling to the Open Days

Parking
Parking spaces are free to use on campus on the Open Day, however please note that dangerous, illegal and inappropriate parking is not tolerated.

Public Transport
For details of other transport links to Maynooth, please read our location information .

Campus Tours

Our Student Ambassadors host tours of both campuses and tell you about our beautiful historic buildings, new facilities and their own experiences of life at Maynooth University. Tours typically last 45 minutes and incorporate a 10 minute tour of the Library.
Check out our campus before you arrive.
