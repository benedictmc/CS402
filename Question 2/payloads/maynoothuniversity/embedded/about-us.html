Maynooth University is an internationally recognised institution located 25 kilometres outside of Dublin, Ireland, and is the nations fastest growing university.

One of four constituent universities of the National University of Ireland, Maynooth University in 2018 placed in the global top 100 universities under 50 years old in the Times Higher Education World University Rankings.

On 16 June 2017, Maynooth University celebrated its 20th birthday, having been formally established as an autonomous university in 1997. Yet, it traces its origins to the foundation of the Royal College of St. Patrick in 1795, drawing inspiration from a heritage that includes over 200 years of education and scholarship.

Today, Maynooth University is a place of lively contrastsa modern institution, dynamic, rapidly-growing, research-led and engaged, yet grounded in historic academic strengths and scholarly traditions.

With more than 12,000 students from more than 90 countries, Maynooth offers a range of programmes at undergraduate, Masters and PhD level in the humanities, science and engineering, and social sciences, including business, law, and education. The University also offers a range of international programmes and partnerships.

Maynooths unique collegial culture fosters an interdisciplinary approach to research, which its world-class academics bring to bear in tackling some of the most fundamental challenges facing society today. The Universitys research institutes and centres consolidate and deliver this impact as vibrant communities of learning, discovery and creation. Research at Maynooth also is very much central to its teaching, and the University prides itself on placing equal value on its research and teaching missions.

In addition to being named in THEs Top 50 Under 50, Maynooth University is recognised among the top 400 universities in the world, the top 200 European universities, and as one of the top 200 universities for international connections and outlook.

Mar cheann de cheithre cinn de chomhcholist de chuid Ollscoil na hireann, in 2017 bh Ollscoil Mh Nuad i measc an 100 ollscoil is fearr ar domhan ar ollscoileanna iad at nos l n 50 bliain ar an bhfd, de rir Rang Domhanda ar Ollscoileanna a rinne Times Higher Education.

Ar an 16 Meitheamh 2017, rinne Ollscoil Mh Nuad ceiliradh ar 20 bliain a bunaithe. Bunaodh Ollscoil Mh Nuad go foirmiil mar ollscoil fhinrialaitheach in 1997. Ach i ndirre, tann stair na hollscoile siar chomh fada le bun Choliste Roga Naomh Pdraig in 1795 agus faigheann s inspiorid oidhreacht a chuimsonn breis agus 200 bliain doideachas agus de scolireacht.
Sa l at inniu ann, feictear codarsnacht bhromhar in Ollscoil Mh Nuad institiid nua-aimseartha, fuinniil, at ag fs go tapa agus at gafa le taighde agus drithe ar an taighde sin ach ag an am canna at framhaithe i mbuanna acadla stairila agus i dtraidisin na scolireachta.

T nos m n 12,000 mac linn as breis agus 90 tr ag freastal ar Ollscoil Mh Nuad agus cuireann s rimse clr ar fil ag leibhal fochime, Mistreachta agus PhD sna daonnachta, san eolaocht agus san innealtireacht, agus sna heolaochta sisialta lena n-irtear gn, dl agus oideachas. Chomh maith leis sin, cuireann an Ollscoil rimse de chlir agus de chomhphirtochta idirnisinta ar fil.

Cothaonn an cultr uathil colisteach at in Ollscoil Mh Nuad cur chuige idirdhisciplneach maidir le taighde agus baineann na hacadir den chad scoth at ag an ollscoil feidhm as an gcur chuige sin chun aghaidh a thabhairt ar chuid de na dshlin is m at le sr sa tsocha sa l at inniu ann. Danann institiid agus lrionaid taighde na hOllscoile tionchar an chultir sin a dhaingni agus a sholthar agus iad ag feidhmi mar phobail bhromhara a chuireann an lann, an fhionnachtain agus an chruthaitheacht chun cinn. Ina theannta sin, t pirt thar a bheith tbhachtach ag an taighde a dhantar in Ollscoil Mh Nuad i rl teagaisc na hollscoile agus is cis bhrid don ollscoil go gcuirtear an luach canna ar na misin at aici i dtaca le taighde agus teagasc.

Chomh maith le bheith ainmnithe ag THE i measc an 50 ollscoil is fearr faoi 50 bliain daois, t Ollscoil Mh Nuad irithe i measc an 400 ollscoil is fearr ar domhan, i measc an 200 ollscoil is fearr san Eoraip, agus ar cheann den 200 ollscoil is fearr maidir le naisc agus dearcadh idirnisinta.
