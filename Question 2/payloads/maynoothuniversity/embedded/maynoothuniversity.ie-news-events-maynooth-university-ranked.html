Maynooth University ranked 80th in top Young Universities in the world

Wednesday, June 6, 2018 - 15:00

Irish Version

Maynooth University has sustained its place in the top 100 global universities under 50 years old, achieving a ranking of #80 in the latest Times Higher Education (THE) Young University Rankings .

The Young University Rankings are based on a range of criteria, including number and quality of scholarly papers and citations, research income achieved, reputation for research and teaching, number of PhDs awarded, and number of international staff and students.

Earlier this year, Maynooth University also was listed amongst the Top 200 institutions worldwide in the QS World University Rankings by Subject for two of its departments: English and Geography , both of which ranked in the 151-200 range.

Maynooth University President, Professor Philip Nolan , commented: It has been just over 20 years since Maynooth University was established as an independent institution, and to be ranked amongst the Top 100 Young Universities, as well as having two of our departments make it to the Top 200 in the QS World University Rankings by Subject, is a real endorsement of the hard work, research impact, and exceptional teaching of our staff.

Commenting on Maynooth Universitys ranking, Phil Baty, Times Higher Education World University Rankings Editor, said: Congratulations to Maynooth University for making the top 100 in the 2018 list of the worlds leading young universities.

The Times Higher Education Young University Rankings use the same exacting and tough 13 performance indicators as the traditional THE World University Rankings, covering teaching, research, knowledge transfer and international outlook, so being in the world top 100 is an exceptional achievement.

Tracing its origins to the foundation of the Royal College of St Patrick in 1795, Maynooth University was formally established as an autonomous university in 1997. Maynooth is one of Irelands fastest growing university with more than 12,000 students, including almost 1,600 taught postgraduate and professional students and over 400 research students.

An 80 hit bainte amach ag Ollscoil Mh Nuad sa rang ar na hOllscoileanna gais fearr ar domhan

6 Meitheamh, 2018 : T a hit coinnithe ag Ollscoil Mh Nuad i measc an 100 ollscoil is fearr ar domhan faoi 50 bliain daois, agus an t-ochtd hit bainte amach aici sa rang is dana at danta ag Times Higher Education (THE) ar ollscoileanna ga.

Bunatear Rang na nOllscoileana ga ar rimse critar lena n-irtear lon agus caighden na bpipar scolrtha agus na ndeismireachta bhaill foirne, ioncam a bailodh thaighde, an cl at ar thaighde agus ar theagasc, lon nandochtireachta a bronnadh, agus lon na mball foirne idirnisinta agus lon na mac linn idirnisinta.

Nos luaithe i mbliana, dirigh le hOllscoil Mh Nuad it a bhaint amach ar an liosta de na 200 institiid is fearr i Ranguithe Ollscoile an Domhain QS de rir bhair in dh roinn: Roinn an Bharla agus Roinn na Treolaochta . Dirigh leis na ranna sin it a bhaint amach sa rang sa rimse 151-200.

Dirt Uachtarn Ollscoil Mh Nuad, an tOllamh Pilib Nuallin : Nl ann ach dreach breis agus fiche bliain bunaodh Ollscoil Mh Nuad mar institiid neamhsplech faoi Acht na nOllscoileanna, 1997. Timid ar cheann de na 100 Ollscoil g is fearr agus dirigh le dh roinn de chuid na hOllscoile it a bhaint amach sa chad 200 ollscoil i Ranguithe Ollscoile an Domhain QS de rir bhair. Is moladh mr an t-aitheantas seo don obair dhian, do thionchar an taighde, agus don teagasc den scoth at danta ag r bhfoireann.

Ag labhairt faoin rath at ar Ollscoil Mh Nuad sa rang, dirt Phil Baty, eagarthir Rang Ollscoile Times Higher Education : Comhghairdeas le hOllscoil Mh Nuad as it a bhaint amach sa chad 100 ollscoil ar liosta 2018 de na hollscoileanna ga is fearr ar domhan.

Baineann Rang Ollscoileanna ga Times Higher Education sid as na tr cinn dag channa de thscair feidhmochta agus a sidtear sa Rang traidisinta ar Ollscoileanna an Domhain THE. Cuirtear teagasc, taighde, aistri eolais agus dearcadh idirnisinta san ireamh iontu. Mar sin, acht suntasach at ann it a bhaint amach sna chad 100 ollscoil sa rang sin.

Tann oidhreacht na hOllscoile siar go dt bun Choliste Roga Naomh Pdraig sa bhliain 1795 ach bunaodh Ollscoil Mh Nuad go foirmiil mar ollscoil finrialaitheach sa bhliain 1997. Is Ollscoil Mh Nuad an ollscoil is gasta fs in irinn. T breis agus 12,000 mac linn ag freastal uirthi, lena n-irtear 1,600 mac linn a bhfuil iarchim teagaisc ar bun acu n ar mic linn proifisinta iad, agus breis agus 400 mac linn taighde.
